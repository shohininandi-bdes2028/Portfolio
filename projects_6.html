Interactive Double Sided Zine telling the story of Kumb Mela

The front of the zine tells the story of why kumb mela happens in Nashik. The illustrations and colours are bright and cheerful as its for the age group of 10-12years kids. Some pages have pop-ups, highlighting the main characters.

The back side of the zine consists of a game called Moksh Patam the old version of snake and ladders, where the ladders symbolysis good deeds and the snakes the bad deeds. It is made so that the kids can play it after reading the zine.

Beside the game board, the rituals of kumb mela are shown through warli art, the traditional art style of maharastra.

A bag was also made to keep the zine in it

Book Designed based on storytelling through stained glass

Its a glossary based on the understanding on how stain glasses in churches and cathedrals help in storytelling the next generation about the values, morals and gods beliefs.

The colours are chosen based on the topic, black gives contrast to the stain glass, ultramarine blue for the colour is used frequently. The light grey is used to give contrast to the other pages.


Fonts used are:

Letters-Metalic Gothic

Sub Heads-Apple Garamond

Body-Garamond

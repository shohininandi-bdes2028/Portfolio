Cards Designed on the font Gill Sans

Gill Sans was used a lot in british railways and in publishing like the Penguin books, it was also known as Helvetica of England, therefore these cards were designed with britsih aesthetics.

Its a card game of guessing the font based on the clueses provided on the card about the anatomy of the respective letter.

The manual and the packaging od the cards were also made, the packaging is based on the newspaper style and contents all the history of the font.
